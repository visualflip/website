Young Lions is one of the biggest creativity festivals of the world, it gathers all the new talent of the advertising and communication industry, giving them the opportunity to win and participate in the Cannes Lions Festival. I was invited to design the festival's identity, which will represent the best young Colombian talent in Cannes Lions, France. The design is based on the fearless attitude that every competitor must have. No matter the category, in order to win the gold lion the work has to be brave and Intrepid.—
